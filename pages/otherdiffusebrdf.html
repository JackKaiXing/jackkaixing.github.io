BRDF: Any other diffuse BRDF?

Lambertian diffuse brdf is commonly used, which is developed in 1760, more than two hundred years ago. Is there anything better?


As the wiki of Oren–Nayar reflectance model points out that "The Lambertian model is an inadequate approximation of the diffuse component. This is primarily because the Lambertian model does not take the roughness of the surface into account."
